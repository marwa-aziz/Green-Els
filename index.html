Vision

We envision a sustainable community where environmental responsibility, mutual respect, and integrity are embedded in everyday practices. Through unified recycling efforts and strong environmental leadership, we aim to create lasting positive change that protects natural resources and supports future generations.

Reduce

Reducing waste means using fewer resources and making thoughtful choices in our daily lives. By reducing consumption, avoiding unnecessary packaging, and reusing items whenever possible, we help protect the environment, save energy, and minimize pollution. Reduce is the first step toward a cleaner and more sustainable future

Reuse

Reusing items helps extend their life and reduces the amount of waste sent to landfills. By using materials again such as containers, bags, and clothes we save natural resources, reduce pollution, and support a more sustainable environment. Reuse encourages creativity, responsibility, and smart consumption

Recycle

Recycling helps protect our environment by reducing waste, saving natural resources, and minimizing pollution. By reusing materials such as paper, plastic, and metal, we conserve energy and support a more sustainable future. Recycling is a shared responsibility that encourages smarter choices and helps build environmentally conscious communities for future generations

ELS Goes Green

Join the Recycling Movement in Egypt! Every piece of plastic, paper, or carton you recycle helps protect our cities, our Nile, and our future. Egypt is working hard to build a greener tomorrow the national waste management plan aims to raise the municipal recycling rate from around 37 % today to 60 % by 2027, turning more waste into reusable materials and energy while supporting the circular economy. EgyptToday Every small effort counts separate, collect, and recycle to keep Egypt clean, create new jobs, and make our planet healthier for generations to come!
